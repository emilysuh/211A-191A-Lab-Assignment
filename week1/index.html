Emily is a Seoul-born, SoCal-raised, Oakland-living 20-something. She enjoys reading fiction and poetry, consuming copious amounts of American and Korean television, and cooking spice-heavy meals.

As a critical digital map maker, her goals are to support communities in pursuing participatory action research projects. Such research would help communities identify which policies to change and which strategies to prioritize.
